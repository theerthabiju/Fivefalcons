Base Frames & Equipment Skids

For generators, compressors, and other heavy equipment.

  • General Structural Steel Works involve the fabrication and assembly of key structural elements essential for the stability and strength of buildings, bridges, industrial facilities, and other infrastructure projects. This typically includes manufacturing and installing beams, which support horizontal loads; columns, which bear vertical loads and transfer them to foundations; trusses, which are triangular frameworks that provide rigidity and distribute weight efficiently; and bracings, which reinforce structures against lateral forces such as wind or seismic activity. Together, these components form the backbone of safe, durable, and robust steel frameworks used in a wide range of construction applications.
  • Steel Towers & Gantries are engineered to provide reliable support for lighting systems, telecommunication equipment, and other critical infrastructure. These robust steel structures are built to withstand environmental stress and heavy loads. Steel towers often rise high to maximize coverage and visibility, whereas gantries span horizontally to support overhead equipment or traffic signage, ensuring safety and functionality in industrial, commercial, and public spaces.
  • Mezzanine Floors are custom-designed and engineered for heavy-duty use in warehouses, factories, and industrial facilities, creating additional usable space without costly building extensions. Made from durable steel, these intermediate floors can support storage racks, equipment, or office space, significantly increasing operational capacity and improving workflow efficiency.
  • Shoring Towers are temporary yet crucial load-bearing structures used during construction or renovation projects. They support concrete slabs, beams, or entire structural sections until permanent supports are in place and the concrete has set. Engineered for stability and safety, they help prevent collapse or shifting, ensuring a secure working environment on site.
  • Base Frames & Equipment Skids are robust steel frames fabricated to support and transport heavy machinery like generators, compressors, and pumps. Designed to distribute weight evenly and reduce vibration, these structures simplify installation and relocation of equipment, protect it from damage, and enhance operational stability in demanding industrial environments.
