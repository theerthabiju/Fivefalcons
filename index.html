About Company

Five Falcons Metal Industries Factory

مؤسسة مصنع الصقور الخمسة للصناعات المعدنية

Five Falcons Metal Industries Factory is dedicated to becoming a leading provider of high-quality engineering and metal fabrication solutions in Saudi Arabia, serving the country’s dynamic construction and industrial sectors. Guided by our mission to deliver innovative solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency, we help bring complex projects to life with precision and reliability. Aligned with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 and driven by upcoming global events like the FIFA World Cup 2034, we recognize the growing demand for steel, metal fabrication, and industrial support services. Five Falcons is committed to meeting this demand by offering customized solutions across construction, oil & gas, and manufacturing—building lasting value for our clients and contributing to the Kingdom’s ambitious development goals.
