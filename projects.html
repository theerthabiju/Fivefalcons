Our Projects

Structural Steel Works

Structural steel works involve the fabrication, supply, and installation of steel frameworks that form the backbone of buildings, industrial facilities, bridges, and specialized structures. This includes beams, columns, trusses, mezzanines, platforms, and other load-bearing elements designed for strength, safety, and long-term durability.

Mezzanine floors

Custom metal fabrication includes designing, cutting, shaping, and assembling metal parts to create products tailored to specific needs. Services like bending, welding, laser cutting, and machining produce items such as enclosures, brackets, frames, and decorative panels — all crafted to meet unique project requirements.

Inducstraial And Domestic Chimney

Industrial and domestic chimney fabrication and installation work includes designing, manufacturing, and erecting chimneys of various sizes and materials, ensuring proper ventilation, durability, and compliance with safety standards for factories, commercial buildings, and homes.

Stainless Steel walkway, Handrails & Railings

Stainless steel walkways, handrails, and railings are designed and fabricated to provide safe and durable access solutions. They are corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. Installation covers platforms, staircases, balconies, and industrial work areas.

Cargo Basket &Cage

Cargo and man basket work includes designing, fabricating, and certifying baskets for safe lifting and transport of materials and personnel in industrial and offshore applications. Each basket is built to meet strict safety standards and project-specific requirements.

Lifitng Beams

Lifting beams work includes the design and fabrication of beams used for safe material handling. These beams help distribute loads evenly during lifting operations. They are manufactured to meet specific capacity, size, and safety requirements for industrial use.

Stainless steel kitchen tables, sinks, cookers, and hoods

Stainless steel kitchen tables, sinks, cookers, and hoods work includes the design, fabrication, and installation of durable, hygienic kitchen equipment. These products are ideal for commercial kitchens, hotels, and food processing areas. They are built to meet high standards of cleanliness, safety, and long-lasting performance.

Steel Tower

Steel tower work includes designing, fabricating, and erecting towers for various industrial and commercial applications. This covers communication towers, utility towers, and custom steel structures. Each tower is engineered for strength, stability, and durability to meet specific project
requirements.
